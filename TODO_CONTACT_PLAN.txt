CONTACT + LOCAL BUSINESS IMPROVEMENT PLAN (for BlueWave Clothing)

Goal
Turn the current marketing website into a tool that generates leads for local shops by adding: reliable contact submissions, instant call/WhatsApp actions, and basic catalogue/enquiry support.

1) Must-have: Real Contact Form Submissions
Current Issue
- contact form in templates/contact.html is demo-only (action="#" + onsubmit alert).

Add
- Backend route: POST /contact
- Server-side validation: name, email, subject (optional), message
- Persistence option (choose later):
  A) Store submissions in local SQLite (recommended for offline/local ops)
  B) Send email to store.email (optional)
- UX: show “Thanks, we received your message” on success; show error on failure.

2) High-impact: One-tap Contact Actions everywhere
Add/Update on all pages (header/footer/floating button)
- Click-to-call: tel:+91XXXXXXXXXX
- WhatsApp: https://wa.me/<number>?text=<prefilled message>
- Google Maps link to the store address.

3) Next iteration: Catalogue / Enquiry flow
Current Issue
- Men/Women/Kids pages are mostly landing pages.

Add
- Small product catalogue per category (even static starter data is fine)
- Product cards: name, price range, tags (new/discount/best)
- “Enquire about this” button that routes to /contact with product prefilled in the message.

4) Trust + SEO basics
Add
- Meta tags: title + description + Open Graph for better sharing
- Business trust section:
  - location
  - timing
  - contact methods
  - optional reviews/testimonials
- Cleaner heading structure (H1/H2) and descriptive text.

5) Optional: Open now / closed now indicator
Use store timing + server time (or client-side) to display:
- “Open now” or “Closed now”

Implementation Order (recommended)
Step 1: Implement POST /contact + update contact form to submit to it
Step 2: Add success/failure UI for submissions
Step 3: Replace WhatsApp button + add tel and maps links
Step 4 (later): Add product catalogue + enquiry prefill

Notes
- For local shops, lead capture + quick contact links are the top conversion drivers.
- Storage (SQLite) is operationally simpler than email for many small businesses.
